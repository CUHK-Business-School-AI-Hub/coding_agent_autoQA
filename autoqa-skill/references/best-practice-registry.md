## Candidate Packs

## Adding a Pack

Create a direct child of `references/` named `best-practice-<scope>.md` from `assets/templates/BEST-PRACTICE-PACK.md`. Add it to the table above only after its promotion criteria are met. Link it directly from `SKILL.md` when Active so progressive disclosure does not depend on a deep reference chain.

An expired pack is not Active for planning purposes. Research its claims again and either refresh, supersede, or remove it from the Active table.
